Chemequal: a tool for balancing chemical equations.

Instructions
Simply type the left side of the formula into the 'eq1' box, and the right side into the 'eq2' box. Then hit submit and Chemequal will calculate the values necessary to balance the equation.

Examples
Taken from my science homework

H2+O2-->H2O outputs 2H2+O2-->2H2O
N2+H2-->NH3 outputs N2+3H2-->2NH3
CH4+O2-->CO2+H2O outputs CH4+2O2->CO2+2H2O


Limitations:
  • Cannot calculate results involving a coefficient greater than 9
  • Cannot parse polyatomic ions (eg. (SO4)2) as of now. To use polyatomic ions, distribute the subscripts.
  • Cannot be fed after midnight. I'm still working on that bug.
